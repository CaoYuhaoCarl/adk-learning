Most recently, on March 23, 2026, NVIDIA and Emerald AI partnered with major energy companies to develop "AI factories" that will connect to the grid, generate AI tokens, and operate as flexible energy assets, utilizing the new NVIDIA Vera Rubin DSX AI Factory reference design.
